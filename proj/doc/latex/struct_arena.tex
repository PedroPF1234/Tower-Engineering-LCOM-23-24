\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Struct that represents an \doxylink{struct_arena}{Arena}. 

Struct that contains the background sprite, the decorations, the towers, the shop and the player base.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em background} & The background sprite of the arena. \\
\hline
{\em decorations} & An array of \doxylink{struct_animated_game_object}{Animated\+Game\+Object} pointers that represent the animated decorations on the arena. \\
\hline
{\em towers} & The array of Tower objects that are present in the arena. \\
\hline
{\em shop} & The shop object that is present in the arena. \\
\hline
{\em base} & The player base object that is present in the arena. \\
\hline
{\em num\+\_\+targets} & The number of target coordinates that the enemies will follow. \\
\hline
{\em num\+\_\+decorations} & The number of decorations in the arena (Although variable, it is always 3). \\
\hline
{\em target\+\_\+coordinates} & The array of the coordinates that the enemies will follow. \\
\hline
{\em spawn\+\_\+x} & The x coordinate of the spawn point of the enemies. \\
\hline
{\em spawn\+\_\+y} & The y coordinate of the spawn point of the enemies. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}

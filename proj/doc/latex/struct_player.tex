\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Struct that represents the player. 

Struct that contains the player game object, the sprites, the current direction, the position, the speed, the origin offset, the hit points and the weapon status.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em player} & The player game object. \\
\hline
{\em sprites} & The array of Animated\+Sprites that represent the player walking in different directions. \\
\hline
{\em current\+\_\+direction} & The current direction of the player. \\
\hline
{\em x} & The x position of the player. \\
\hline
{\em y} & The y position of the player. \\
\hline
{\em speed} & The array representing speed of the player in the x and y directions. \\
\hline
{\em origin\+\_\+offset\+\_\+x} & The offset of the origin of the player sprite in the x direction. \\
\hline
{\em origin\+\_\+offset\+\_\+y} & The offset of the origin of the player sprite in the y direction. \\
\hline
{\em hit\+\_\+points} & The current hit points of the player. \\
\hline
{\em max\+\_\+hit\+\_\+points} & The maximum hit points of the player. \\
\hline
{\em has\+Weapon} & A boolean that represents if the player has a weapon. \\

\end{DoxyParams}
